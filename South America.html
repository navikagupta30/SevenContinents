Let's take a trip to South America!

South America is home to the largest flying bird in the world - the Andean Condor. The bird, a vulture, is a national symbol of Chile, Ecuador and Peru and sits atop the coat of arms of Colombia. There is a rich variety of music in South America. A few of the popular genres include samba from Brazil, tango from Argentina & Uruguay, and cumbia from Colombia. The continent is home to some of the most interesting and unique species of animals including the llama, anaconda, piranha, jaguar, vicu√±a, and tapir. Covering around 2.7 million square miles, the Amazon is the largest rain forest in the world. The second longest river in the world after the Nile, the Amazon river is the largest by volume of water discharged. Brazil, the country which is the largest coffee producer in the world, is in South America.
